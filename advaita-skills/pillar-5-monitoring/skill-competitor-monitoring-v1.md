## Purpose
Weekly monitoring of key FKI competitors and franchise broker industry trends. AI surfaces intelligence that informs FKI's positioning, pricing, and marketing strategy.

## Monitored Competitors (general category — specific names not listed here)
- Top franchise broker networks
- Portal-based franchise matching services
- Independent franchise consultants with significant online presence

## Monitoring Sources (REVERSIBLE — read-only web monitoring)
- Google Alerts for competitor brand names
- Google Search rankings for target keywords ("franchise consultant," "franchise broker," "how to buy a franchise")
- Social media activity monitoring (LinkedIn, Instagram)
- Review profile monitoring (Google, Yelp, Trustpilot)
