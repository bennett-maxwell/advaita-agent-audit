# Weekly Business Review Automation Skill v1

## Purpose
Every Monday at 8:00 AM MDT, AI compiles and posts the full weekly business review. Covers every critical business dimension. Bennett reads it in 5 minutes and is fully current.

## Weekly Review Sections

**Revenue (from QB sync)**
- Week revenue vs. prior week vs. goal
- MTD and YTD status
- Consult your accountant for financial planning; these figures are from QB API and for operational visibility only

**Pipeline**
- Active candidates by stage
- New leads this week
- Closes this week and expected next 2 weeks
- Pipeline value (estimated; see pipeline value report skill)

**Marketing**
- Meta Ads: spend, CPL, lead volume vs. prior week
- Organic: sessions, top pages, conversion rate

**Operations**
- Work orders completed by Leo/Ivan this week
- Skills updated or created
- Sprint Board: completed vs. open items

**Franchise Portfolio**
- IC/SH/SRP: any new territory openings, news, FDD updates
- Territory availability changes
- Franchisee milestones (awards, openings, anniversaries)

**Advaita Progress**
- Autonomy gap % estimate: what percentage of FKI operations are now AI-autonomous
- New capabilities added this week
- Remaining gap areas

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Weekly leadership meeting or manual dashboard review.
**Result:** Full business health check delivered automatically every Monday. Bennett never blind about any dimension.
**Advaita gap closed:** Weekly business visibility — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
