# Re-Engagement Trigger Skill v1

## Purpose
Automatically re-engage dormant candidates at the right moment with the right message. No broker intervention required.

## Dormancy Triggers

**30-Day Dormant**: Candidate went quiet after initial contact.
→ Sequence: Educational email (franchise process overview) + soft check-in → 3 days later: territory availability update (if relevant) → 7 days later: relevant brand news or FDD update

**60-Day Dormant**: Candidate went quiet post-discovery call.
→ Sequence: "Things change" re-intro + updated brand shortlist → 5 days later: "Is now the right time?" reflection prompt → 10 days later: final offer to reconnect with broker

**90-Day Dormant**: Long-cold prospect.
→ Trigger: Annual "franchise landscape update" newsletter
→ Event-based: New territory opening near candidate's market
→ Life event signals: LinkedIn job change, relocation detected

**Seasonal Re-Engagement**: January (New Year decisions), October (Q4 bonus season), June (mid-year financial review).

## Message Personalization Variables
All messages pull from GHL: candidate name, last brand discussed, their stated motivation, geographic market.

## AI Decision on Re-Engagement Type
- If last contact reason was CAPITAL → financial education sequence
- If last contact reason was TIMELINE → milestone reminder sequence
- If last contact reason was SPOUSE → family decision support sequence
- If last contact reason unknown → general re-engagement

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker manually digging through CRM to find dormant leads.
**Result:** Every dormant lead gets touched at optimal intervals. Revenue recovered from "lost" pipeline.
**Advaita gap closed:** Dormant lead recovery — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
