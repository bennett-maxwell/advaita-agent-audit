# Salad House Buyer Matcher Skill v1

## Purpose
Salad House is a high-AUV food franchise with operational intensity. AI matches candidates who can execute at the level the brand requires.

## SH Ideal Buyer Profile
- Owner-operator orientation (or strong GM plan with operational oversight)
- Comfortable in food service environment (doesn't need culinary skill, but can't be food-averse)
- Investor/ROI orientation is appropriate and encouraged
- SBA-eligible profile preferred (SH is SBA-approved)
- Investment range: per FDD Item 7 (results may vary; see FDD)
- AUV: brand-reported figure available (cite source when presenting; results may vary; see FDD)

## SH State Blocks (HARD)
SH territories not registered in: CT, IL, MN, WA
If candidate's target state is on this list → SH is removed from recommendation.

## SH Operational Reality
High volume, customer-facing, food service brand. Year 1 requires significant operator attention. NOT a recommendation for candidates who expect passive income from Day 1.

## SH Discovery Topics
- Prior food service or high-volume retail experience? (not required, but helps)
- GM candidate identified or plan to hire?
- SBA pre-qualification status?
- Market research on competition in target territory?

## AUV Framing (always FDD-qualified)
"Salad House has strong reported unit economics — per their FDD Item 19 (results may vary; see FDD for details). The system is designed for motivated operators who are willing to be engaged especially in Year 1."

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker presenting SH to passive income seekers who'll struggle.
**Result:** SH presented only to operationally-capable, motivated buyers. Strong franchisee outcomes.
**Advaita gap closed:** SH-specific matching — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
