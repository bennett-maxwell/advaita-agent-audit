# Prior Business Experience Evaluator Skill v1

## Purpose
Candidates with prior business experience can handle more operationally complex franchises. AI identifies experience level and adjusts brand complexity recommendations accordingly.

## Experience Tiers

**Tier 0 — No Prior Business Ownership**
Profile: Corporate employee only, no entrepreneurial background
Brand Recommendation: Highly systematized franchises with comprehensive training, strong franchisor support, lower operational complexity
FKI fit: IC (community hub, manager-run, strong system), SRP (event-based, structured model)
Needs: More hand-holding in discovery, extra emphasis on training/support in brand presentation

**Tier 1 — Side Business / Freelance**
Profile: Has run something small (freelance, consulting, side hustle) but not a full business
Brand Recommendation: Moderate complexity, strong support, growth-oriented systems
FKI fit: IC, SRP, SH (if comfortable with food service)
Needs: Emphasis on going from side hustle to scalable system

**Tier 2 — Prior Business Ownership (small / single location)**
Profile: Has owned and operated a small business or single-location enterprise
Brand Recommendation: Full range including more operationally demanding concepts
FKI fit: All brands including SH
Needs: Frame franchise as upgrade — proven system replaces DIY guesswork

**Tier 3 — Multi-Location / Serial Entrepreneur**
Profile: Has managed multiple locations, sold businesses, or led significant teams
Brand Recommendation: Multi-unit emphasis, area developer conversations, high-AUV brands
FKI fit: All brands, multi-unit emphasis
Needs: Frame franchise as leverage on existing skills + wealth building vehicle

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker giving same discovery experience to Tier 0 and Tier 3 candidates.
**Result:** Every candidate gets experience-calibrated discovery. Advanced candidates aren't bored; new candidates aren't overwhelmed.
**Advaita gap closed:** Experience-calibrated matching — from 0% → 90% AI-autonomous.
