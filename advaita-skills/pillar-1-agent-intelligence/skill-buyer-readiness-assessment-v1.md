# Buyer Readiness Assessment Skill v1

## Purpose
Assess whether a candidate is emotionally AND logistically ready to move to award. Prevents premature push (which kills deals) and delayed push (which loses candidates to competitors).

## Readiness Matrix

### Emotional Readiness Signals (from call notes, email tone, engagement)
- HIGH: Asking specific operational questions, sharing with spouse, researching territory
- MEDIUM: Still asking "what if" questions, comparing many brands
- LOW: Still in information-gather mode, not asking next steps

### Logistical Readiness Signals
- Funding source identified and accessible? YES / NO / IN PROGRESS
- Spouse / partner aligned? YES / NO / UNKNOWN
- Timeline confirmed and realistic? YES / NO
- Legal counsel engaged for FDD review? YES / NO (prompt if no)
- Validation calls completed? YES / NO (prompt if no)

## Readiness Score
5/5 criteria met → PUSH TO CLOSE: schedule award call
3-4/5 → CONDITIONAL ADVANCE: address 1–2 remaining criteria
1-2/5 → DEVELOP: specific nurture actions for each unmet criterion
0/5 → PAUSE: candidate not ready; move to low-touch sequence

## Action Triggers
PUSH TO CLOSE → broker notification + award call scheduling
CONDITIONAL ADVANCE → AI generates specific outreach to address gaps
DEVELOP → AI activates targeted nurture content
PAUSE → AI sends "circle back in 30 days" touchpoint

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker manually judging when to push.
**Result:** AI identifies the exact right moment to escalate. Fewer dropped deals.
**Advaita gap closed:** Deal timing intelligence — from 0% → 85% AI-autonomous.
