# Brand News Brief Skill v1

## Purpose
Daily/weekly monitoring of FKI franchise brands (IC, SH, SRP) for news, new store openings, awards, press, and FDD updates. Broker always has current talking points.

## Monitoring Sources
- Brand social media (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- Franchisor press releases and newsletters
- Google News alerts for brand names
- FDD update notifications (annual FDD updates trigger re-review)

## Brief Content (weekly, Monday delivery)

For each brand:
- New franchise openings this week (where, who)
- Any press or awards received
- Brand growth trajectory (unit count trend)
- Any FDD update (annual issuance alert)
- New franchisee validation contacts available (Item 20 update)

## Usage in Discovery
When broker has a candidate exploring IC, SH, or SRP:
AI surfaces relevant news from brief into discovery call prep (skill-discovery-call-prep-v1):
"IC just opened their Nth location in [state] — strong growth signal worth mentioning"

## FDD Update Alert
When brand issues updated FDD (annual): AI flags all active candidates in that brand's pipeline to confirm they have the current version before any FDD review period begins.

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker manually searching for brand updates.
**Result:** Every broker conversation includes fresh, specific brand momentum data. Credibility and conviction increase.
**Advaita gap closed:** Brand intelligence monitoring — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
