# Funding Path Recommendation Skill v1

## Purpose
Given candidate's financial profile, AI recommends the optimal funding path(s) for franchise investment. Replaces broker manually remembering all funding options.

## Funding Path Decision Matrix

**Input**: Liquid capital, net worth, retirement account balance, home equity (approximate), credit score range, timeline.

**Path 1: Cash / Liquid Investment**
Best for: Candidates with liquid capital ≥ brand minimum (per FDD Item 7; results may vary)
Pros: Fastest, no debt, full ownership
Cons: Full capital exposure
Recommend when: Liquid ≥ total investment per FDD

**Path 2: SBA 7(a) Loan**
Best for: Candidates with 10–20% of total investment liquid, strong credit
Typical structure: Candidate contributes 10–20%, SBA-guaranteed loan covers remainder
SBA Franchise Registry: Confirm brand is listed for streamlined approval
Recommend when: Liquid = 15–30% of total investment, credit score 680+
Note: Loan terms, rates, and requirements vary by lender; consult qualified SBA lender

**Path 3: ROBS (Rollover for Business Startups)**
Best for: Candidates with retirement accounts (401k/IRA/403b) above minimum thresholds (varies; consult ROBS provider)
Allows using retirement funds without early withdrawal penalty
Requires custodian setup (3rd party required — refer to specialists)
Recommend when: Retirement balance ≥ brand minimum investment; minimal liquid cash
Note: ROBS has ongoing compliance requirements; consult qualified ROBS provider

**Path 4: HELOC**
Best for: Homeowners with significant equity, low first-mortgage balance
Recommend when: Home equity ≥ 40% of investment needed; flexible draw timeline
Note: Secured against home; consult financial advisor before proceeding

**Path 5: Hybrid / Combination**
Common structure: ROBS + SBA, or Cash + SBA
Recommend when: No single path covers full investment; combination fills gap

## Output
AI generates: recommended path(s) ranked by fit, specific action to take for each path, referral resources for SBA lenders / ROBS custodians.
