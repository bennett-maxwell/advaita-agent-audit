# Conversion Rate Optimizer Skill v1

## Purpose
Continuously analyze where candidates drop out of the FKI pipeline and surface specific, actionable improvements to close rate.

## Funnel Stage Analysis

Track conversion rate at each stage:
- Lead Intake → First Contact: target 80%+ (dropped leads = missed timing or bad data)
- First Contact → Discovery Scheduled: target 40%+ 
- Discovery Scheduled → Discovery Completed: target 85%+
- Discovery → FDD Requested: target 60%+
- FDD → Validation Calls: target 70%+
- Validation → Award: target 50%+

## Drop-Off Diagnosis Protocol

When conversion rate at any stage falls below target for 2+ consecutive weeks:
1. Pull all candidates who dropped at that stage in the period
2. Analyze: common characteristics, source, broker, brand discussed
3. Identify pattern: Is it specific to one broker? One brand? One lead source?
4. Generate hypothesis: "Drop-off at Discovery → FDD is high for SH leads from paid traffic — candidates may be miscalibrated on investment range"
5. Recommend specific fix: "Add investment range FDD qualifier to ad creative"

## Weekly Conversion Report
Posted to #leo-auto every Monday:
- Conversion rate by stage (vs. 4-week average)
- Top 3 candidates to prioritize this week
- 1 conversion optimization hypothesis to test

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker and Bennett reviewing pipeline manually to find problems.
**Result:** AI catches conversion drops within days, not months. Pipeline optimizes continuously.
