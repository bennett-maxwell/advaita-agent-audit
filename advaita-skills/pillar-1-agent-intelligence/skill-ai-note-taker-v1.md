# AI Note-Taker Skill v1 (Discovery Calls)

## Purpose
Capture structured intelligence from every discovery call without broker taking notes. Broker focuses 100% on the human connection. AI handles the capture.

## Note Structure (auto-populated)

**Call Metadata**: Date, duration, broker, candidate, brands discussed

**Key Quotes** (verbatim phrases that signal emotion, objection, or commitment):
Examples: "My wife is really excited about this" / "I'm not sure about the timeline" / "That's exactly what I'm looking for"

**Financial Updates**: Any new capital, funding, or net worth information disclosed

**Lifestyle Data**: Any new information about hours, travel, family, current job

**Objections Raised**: Verbatim + category tag

**Positive Signals**: Enthusiasm, specific questions, next steps they initiated

**Candidate Next Steps Agreed**: What candidate committed to do before next touchpoint

**Broker Next Steps**: What broker committed to do

**Overall Call Score**: HOT / WARM / DEVELOPING / COOLING

## GHL Auto-Update
All structured data auto-populates GHL fields after broker approves (takes <30 seconds).

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Post-call 15–20 minute note session.
**Result:** Broker attention 100% on relationship during call. AI handles all capture and CRM update.
**Advaita gap closed:** Call documentation — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
