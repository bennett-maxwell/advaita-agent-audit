# Lifestyle Alignment Skill v1

## Purpose
The real reason candidates buy — or don't — is lifestyle fit, not financial math. AI maps the candidate's desired lifestyle to franchise reality before presenting any brand.

## Lifestyle Design Framework

**Time Freedom**
- How many hours per week is candidate WILLING to work in Year 1?
- How many hours per week is their GOAL by Year 3?
- Are there hard constraints (childcare pickup, current W2 job, elderly parents)?

**Location**
- Tied to one specific city / neighborhood?
- Open to adjacent markets?
- Any travel tolerance for training / conferences?

**Income Timeline**
- Has 12–24 months of living expenses covered independent of franchise income?
- Or needs franchise cash flow within 6 months? (changes brand recommendation dramatically)

**Identity / Meaning**
- Does candidate want to be known in their community as a business owner? (IC / SRP fit)
- Or is this purely a financial vehicle? (different brand profile)
- Do they care what the business DOES or just that it generates returns?

**Lifestyle Brand Matching**
Answers above feed directly into brand-fit-matching-deep-v1 cultural alignment scoring.

## The "Life Design" Frame
FKI's key differentiator: we design a business that fits your life, not a life that fits a business. This skill operationalizes that frame — AI captures it, stores it, and references it in every subsequent touchpoint.
