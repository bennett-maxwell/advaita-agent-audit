# Healthcare Professional Buyer Profile Skill v1

## Purpose
Healthcare professionals (nurses, pharmacists, physicians, physical therapists) are a high-value FKI buyer segment — high W2 income, disciplined mindset, strong semi-absentee candidate profile.

## Healthcare Buyer Profile
- High W2 income (varies widely by specialty and experience) → strong capital access
- Time-constrained (shift work, on-call) → semi-absentee model is ESSENTIAL
- Risk-averse (medical training = precision, not improvisation) → need strong systems
- Compliance-oriented → franchise system adherence is natural fit
- Community trust and credibility → IC brand alignment (respected local business owner)

## Semi-Absentee Match
Healthcare professionals almost universally need semi-absentee: they cannot leave their career Day 1. IC, SRP are primary matches.

## Discovery Framing
"Healthcare professionals are actually one of our best semi-absentee franchise owners — you have the discipline and community standing to run a great business, and the franchise system handles the daily complexity while you continue your career."

## Financial Profile Notes
W2 income continues through ramp period → capital cushion advantage
May have ROBS-eligible 403b/457b plans in addition to standard 401k
Student loan debt consideration (common in physicians/pharmacists) → net worth vs. liquid capital distinction important
Investment range per FDD Item 7 (results may vary; see FDD for current figures)

## Common Objection for This Segment
"I don't have time." → Address with semi-absentee reality: 5–15 hrs/week after Year 1 ramp.
