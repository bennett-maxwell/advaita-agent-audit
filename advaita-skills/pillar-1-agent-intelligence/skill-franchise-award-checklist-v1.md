# Franchise Award Checklist Skill v1

## Purpose
Once a candidate says yes, AI manages the award process checklist to ensure every step is completed in the right order. Zero missed steps, zero confused candidates.

## Award Process Checklist (sequential)

**Step 1 — Mutual Commitment**
- [ ] Candidate verbal commitment obtained
- [ ] Territory formally held (franchisor confirmation required — route to Leo for human call)
- [ ] Disclosure day documented (FDD Item 2 receipt — 14 days before signing in most states; verify per state law)

**Step 2 — Legal and Financial**
- [ ] Candidate has engaged independent franchise attorney (recommend if not yet done)
- [ ] Funding confirmed and accessible (per pathway identified in skill-funding-path-recommendation-v1)
- [ ] Entity formation initiated (LLC or corporation — recommend consulting attorney)

**Step 3 — FDD Review Period**
- [ ] Candidate has reviewed FDD with attorney
- [ ] All questions answered by franchisor discovery day or final validation calls
- [ ] Candidate confirms understanding of financial projections are estimates (per FDD; results may vary)

**Step 4 — Franchise Agreement**
- [ ] Franchise agreement reviewed by candidate's attorney
- [ ] Signing scheduled
- [ ] Initial fees prepared per FDD Item 5

**Step 5 — Onboarding**
- [ ] Training dates scheduled with franchisor
- [ ] Location search initiated (if applicable)
- [ ] GHL stage updated to "Awarded"

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker manually tracking each award step.
**Result:** Zero award processes fall apart from missed steps. Every deal closes cleanly.
**Advaita gap closed:** Award process management — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
