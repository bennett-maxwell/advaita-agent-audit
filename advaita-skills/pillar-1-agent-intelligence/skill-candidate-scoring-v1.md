# Candidate Scoring Skill v1

## Purpose
Assign every candidate a FranchiseScore (0–100) across 6 dimensions. Score drives brand matching priority, broker time allocation, and pipeline velocity.

## Scoring Dimensions (100 points total)

| Dimension | Max | Scoring Logic |
|-----------|-----|---------------|
| Financial Readiness | 25 | Liquid ≥ brand minimum = 25; ≥ 75% = 18; ≥ 50% = 10; below = 0 |
| Timeline Urgency | 20 | ≤ 6mo = 20; 6–12mo = 15; 12–18mo = 10; 18–24mo = 5; 24mo+ = 0 |
| Motivation Clarity | 20 | Clear W2-exit narrative = 20; income supplement = 14; vague = 7 |
| Operational Fit | 15 | Prior business ownership = 15; management experience = 10; none = 5 |
| Geographic Flexibility | 10 | 3+ metro options = 10; 2 options = 7; 1 locked = 3 |
| Engagement Quality | 10 | Completed full intake = 10; partial = 6; minimal = 2 |

## Score Tiers
- 80–100: HOT — broker priority, 24h response SLA
- 60–79: WARM — standard pipeline, 48h response SLA
- 40–59: DEVELOPING — nurture sequence, monthly check-in
- 0–39: COLD — automated sequence only, no broker time until score rises

## Score Updates
Recalculate after: discovery call, FDD request, validation calls completed, funding confirmed.

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker intuition on who to spend time with.
**Result:** Every broker hour goes to highest-probability candidates. Zero wasted discovery time.
**Advaita gap closed:** Candidate prioritization — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
