# Validation Call Prep Skill v1

## Purpose
Before a candidate speaks with existing franchisees (FDD Item 20 contacts), AI prepares them with the right questions, what to listen for, and what red flags to note. Ensures validation calls move the process forward, not sideways.

## Pre-Validation Brief (AI auto-generates for each candidate)

**What validation calls are for:**
Real conversations with people who made the same decision you're considering. Ask the questions you can't get from the FDD.

**Top 10 Questions to Ask Franchisees:**
1. "What does your typical week look like now vs. in Year 1?"
2. "What surprised you most about the business after opening?"
3. "If you could change one thing about the franchise system, what would it be?"
4. "How long until you reached your target cash flow?"
5. "What does the training and ongoing support look like in practice?"
6. "Would you do it again? What would you do differently?"
7. "How is the relationship with corporate?"
8. "What does your customer acquisition look like — how did you build initial volume?"
9. "What does your team look like? How hard is it to find / keep good people?"
10. "Is the territory protection adequate?"

**What to Listen For (Green Flags):**
- Enthusiasm about the system and support
- Specific positive examples (not just vague praise)
- Proactive mention of strong financial results (per their own FDD Item 19 experience; results vary)

**What to Listen For (Yellow Flags):**
- Hedging on corporate relationship
- Significant variance from FDD Item 19 representations (results may vary; see FDD)
- Staffing / turnover issues

**What to Listen For (Red Flags):**
- Regret or frustration about the decision
- Major operational or support gaps from what was promised
- Difficulty reaching break-even

## Post-Validation Debrief Trigger
After validation calls, AI triggers buyer-readiness-assessment-v1 update and sends broker a summary prompt.
