# Attorney Buyer Profile Skill v1

## Purpose
Attorneys have unique franchise discovery dynamics — they read FDDs analytically, may self-review, and need different framing than other buyers. AI handles attorney-specific discovery.

## Attorney Buyer Profile
- Will read FDD thoroughly (sometimes entire document — be ready)
- May self-represent in FDD review or have trusted franchise attorney
- Analytical, risk-cataloging mindset → needs reassurance, not information overwhelming
- High earning capacity → strong capital position, but high opportunity cost of time
- Partnership structure common (attorney + operational partner)

## FDD Handling for Attorneys
- Don't overpromise simplicity of FDD ("it's straightforward") — they know better
- Provide FDD synthesis (skill-fdd-intake-synthesis-v1) as starting point, not end point
- Explicitly recommend independent franchise attorney review — they'll do it anyway; better to suggest proactively
- Key items attorneys focus on: Item 17 (termination), Item 12 (territory), Item 6 (fees), Item 9 (obligations)

## Discovery Framing
"With your legal background, you'll be more thorough than most buyers in FDD review — which I actually appreciate. The strongest franchisees go in with eyes open. Let me make sure you have everything you need for that process."

## Time / Opportunity Cost Framing
High-earning attorneys: semi-absentee model is essential (their billable hours are too valuable to sacrifice)
Focus on IC's manager-run model — attorney runs the business strategically while continuing legal career

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker intimidated by attorney's analytical questions.
**Result:** Attorney-buyers feel respected and supported through the process. Trust built through competence.
**Advaita gap closed:** Attorney buyer segment — from 0% → 90% AI-autonomous.
