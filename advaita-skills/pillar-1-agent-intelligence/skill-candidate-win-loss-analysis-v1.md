# Candidate Win/Loss Analysis Skill v1

## Purpose
After every deal closes (won) or drops (lost), AI runs structured analysis and adds findings to FKI's collective intelligence. Every outcome makes the next outcome more likely to succeed.

## Win Analysis (deal awarded)

Capture:
- Lead source (which channel, which campaign)
- Time from first contact to award (days)
- Brand awarded and territory
- Candidate profile: capital range, industry, lifestyle goal, buyer type
- Which touchpoints were most impactful (per GHL activity data)
- What objections were raised and how resolved

Pattern mining: "Candidates from LinkedIn organic with healthcare background close at 2x rate of other sources. IC specific."

## Loss Analysis (candidate dropped)

Capture:
- Drop stage (where in pipeline did they leave)
- Last known reason or signal (capital, timeline, spouse, just disappeared)
- Brand(s) that were being presented
- Time invested by broker before drop

Pattern mining: "67% of drops at FDD stage citing capital concern were originally from Google paid — paid traffic may be overselling investment range."

## Monthly Win/Loss Report
Summary posted to #leo-auto:
- Win rate by lead source
- Win rate by brand
- Win rate by broker
- Top 3 learnings from losses
- Top 3 repeatable patterns from wins

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Bennett/brokers reviewing deals intuitively with no structured data.
**Result:** FKI's close rate improves systematically every month based on real data. Compounding advantage.
**Advaita gap closed:** Business intelligence learning loop — from 0% → 90% AI-autonomous.
