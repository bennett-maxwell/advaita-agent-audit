# Military Buyer Profile Skill v1

## Purpose
Military veterans are among the most successful franchise owners. AI identifies veteran candidates and activates VetFran-aware discovery process.

## Military Buyer Profile Signals
- Active duty transitioning out, recently retired, or veteran entrepreneur
- Service branch and rank (officer vs. enlisted informs leadership style)
- Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) → transferable skills mapping
- VA loan awareness (business, not home — VA loans don't fund franchise; clarify early)

## VetFran Program Awareness
Many franchisors offer VetFran fee discounts for honorably discharged veterans (varies by brand — check FDD and ask franchisor). AI flags VetFran-eligible brands in recommendation.

## Military Skill Mapping → Franchise Strengths
- Leadership / team management → owner-operator or multi-unit candidate
- Logistics / operations → systems-driven franchise models
- Mission orientation → IC community hub model (mission = community impact)
- Discipline / follow-the-system mindset → franchise system adherence (key success predictor)

## SBA Veterans Advantage
Reduced SBA guarantee fees for veteran-owned businesses. Route to SBA lenders who specialize in VetFran.

## Discovery Framing
"Your military background is genuinely one of the strongest predictors of franchise success — the ability to follow a proven system, lead a team, and execute under pressure maps directly to what franchisors look for in their best operators."

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker treating veteran candidates like any other lead.
**Result:** Veterans feel recognized and matched specifically. High-intent segment served excellently.
