# Multi-Unit Readiness Assessment Skill v1

## Purpose
Identify candidates with the capital, experience, and mindset for multi-unit franchise ownership. Multi-unit deals generate higher commission and stronger franchisee outcomes.

## Multi-Unit Readiness Signals

**Financial Signals**:
- Liquid capital ≥ 2x single-unit minimum (per FDD Item 7; results may vary)
- Net worth substantially above single-unit threshold
- Access to capital for growth (credit facility, investor partners, cash flow from existing business)

**Experience Signals**:
- Prior business ownership or management of multi-location operations
- Corporate leadership experience (district manager, regional director equivalent)
- Real estate investment background (systems-thinking, location analysis skills)

**Mindset Signals**:
- Explicitly says "I don't want just one" OR "I want to build something bigger"
- Asks about area developer or multi-unit development agreements
- Asks about territory protection for future expansion
- Views franchise as platform, not single job

**Risk Profile**:
- Comfortable with higher initial investment (per FDD; results may vary)
- Long-term wealth building orientation vs. immediate income
- Understands Year 1–2 cash flow implications of multi-unit vs. single-unit start

## Output
MULTI-UNIT CANDIDATE: Flag in GHL, route to highest-experience broker, present multi-unit development agreement options.
SINGLE-UNIT NOW / MULTI LATER: Single unit start with expansion conversation in Year 2.
SINGLE UNIT ONLY: Standard single-unit recommendation.

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker accidentally underselling multi-unit opportunity to qualified candidates.
**Result:** AI surfaces multi-unit candidates. Higher deal value, better franchisee outcomes.
