# New vs. Resale Recommendation Skill v1

## Purpose
When a candidate asks about new vs. resale franchise, AI delivers a structured, data-grounded recommendation based on their specific profile.

## New Unit Pros/Cons
**Pros**: Greenfield territory (yours to build), standard discovery process, franchise-backed training from Day 0, no inherited operational baggage
**Cons**: Starts from zero revenue, longer ramp to cash flow, more uncertainty in early months

## Resale Unit Pros/Cons
**Pros**: Existing customer base and revenue, staff in place, known P&L history, faster cash flow (if healthy unit)
**Cons**: Inherits prior owner's decisions (staffing, reputation, relationships), transfer fees and process, no greenfield territory upside

## Decision Framework by Candidate Profile

**First-time franchise buyer + semi-absentee goal**: New unit (simpler, full system support from Day 1)
**Experienced operator + wants faster cash flow**: Resale worth exploring (if financials are strong)
**Capital-constrained candidate**: New unit typically lower entry cost (per FDD Item 7; results may vary) vs. resale which includes business goodwill premium
**Community-presence goal (IC, SRP)**: New unit preferred (build community identity from Day 1)
**Pure ROI investor**: Evaluate both — resale can have faster IRR if purchased right; new unit has lower acquisition premium

## Red Light on Resale
Never recommend resale without: verified P&L, explained reason for sale, FDD transfer approval confirmation, independent valuation.
