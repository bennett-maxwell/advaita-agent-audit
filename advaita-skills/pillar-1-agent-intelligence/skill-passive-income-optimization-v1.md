# Passive Income Optimization Skill v1

## Purpose
For candidates explicitly seeking passive franchise income (minimal day-to-day involvement), AI identifies the optimal path and sets accurate expectations.

## Passive vs Semi-Absentee Reality Check
TRUE passive franchise ownership (zero involvement) is rare and not ideal. SEMI-ABSENTEE is the realistic goal: 5–15 hours per week after Year 1 ramp.

AI communication: Always clarify this distinction upfront. Candidate expecting zero involvement will be disappointed regardless of brand.

## Passive-Friendly Brand Criteria
- Manager-run model explicitly supported by franchisor
- Strong corporate support for absentee owners
- Proven franchisee success in semi-absentee model
- Low owner-required expertise (concept doesn't require owner's specific skills)

## Passive Income Pathway Optimization

**Step 1**: Confirm candidate accepts semi-absentee reality (5–15 hrs/wk)
**Step 2**: Assess GM hiring plan (existing talent or hiring plan required)
**Step 3**: Match to brands with proven semi-absentee track record
**Step 4**: Model cash flow timeline — when does unit reach positive semi-absentee cash flow? (per FDD Item 19 if disclosed; results may vary; see FDD)
**Step 5**: Multi-unit expansion plan — passive income scales significantly with multiple units

## Financial Framing (always include FDD qualifier)
Semi-absentee income potential varies significantly by brand, territory, operator, and market conditions. See each brand's FDD for any financial performance representations. Results may vary.

**Replaces:** Broker spending call time educating on passive income reality.
**Result:** Candidate arrives at discovery call pre-educated on semi-absentee model. Higher quality conversation.
