# Timeline Alignment Skill v1

## Purpose
Ensure candidate's desired timeline aligns with franchise reality before investing significant discovery time. Timeline mismatches are a top cause of late-stage deal failure.

## Timeline Reality Map (typical franchise process)

| Milestone | Typical Duration |
|-----------|-----------------|
| First contact → Discovery call | 3–7 days |
| Discovery call → FDD receipt | 3–14 days |
| FDD receipt → Validation calls | 7–21 days |
| Validation → Award decision | 14–30 days |
| Award → Training start | 30–90 days (varies by brand) |
| Training → Grand opening | 60–180 days (varies by concept) |
| **Total: First contact → Open doors** | **~6–12 months (typical)** |

(All timelines are approximate and vary by brand, territory, financing, and individual circumstances.)

## Candidate Timeline Check

**If candidate wants to open in < 6 months:**
→ Flag as aggressive. Discuss: is this a hard deadline or a preference?
→ If hard deadline: accelerate discovery, prioritize brands with faster onboarding
→ If preference: educate on realistic timeline, adjust expectation

**If candidate's timeline is 12–24 months:**
→ Standard process. Territory urgency messaging still applies.

**If candidate's timeline is 24+ months:**
→ Move to nurture sequence. Check in at 12-month mark.
→ But: capture and lock territory interest now (territories don't wait)

## W2 Exit Timeline Alignment
If candidate plans to leave job upon franchise award:
- Confirm employer notice period
- Ensure training/opening timeline doesn't conflict with job obligation
- Discuss transition period (many franchisees keep W2 through training + early ramp)

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker discovering timeline conflict at award stage.
**Result:** Zero late-stage timeline surprises. Process flows smoothly.
**Advaita gap closed:** Timeline alignment — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
