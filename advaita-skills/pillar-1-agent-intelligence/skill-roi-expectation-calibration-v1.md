# ROI Expectation Calibration Skill v1

## Purpose
Before award, AI ensures candidate has realistic ROI expectations anchored to FDD data. Prevents the #1 cause of franchisee dissatisfaction: expecting more than the business delivers.

## Expectation Calibration Protocol

**Step 1 — Surface Candidate's Expectation**
Ask (or infer from intake): "What return are you expecting in Year 1? Year 3?"

**Step 2 — Compare to FDD Reality**
For each brand in shortlist:
- If FDD Item 19 is disclosed: present the data as disclosed. "According to their FDD Item 19, franchisees who chose to report... [data]. Results may vary. See FDD for complete details."
- If Item 19 is NOT disclosed: be explicit. "This brand does not include financial performance representations in their FDD. You'll want to rely on validation calls with existing franchisees."

**Step 3 — Timeline Calibration**
Most franchises do not return investment in Year 1. Typical timeline:
- Year 1: Ramp phase — limited or no cash distribution
- Year 2: Stabilization — may reach break-even or modest positive cash flow
- Year 3+: Target operating efficiency — when most franchisees see strongest returns
(All timelines are approximate; actual results vary by brand, territory, operator, and market conditions.)

**Step 4 — Expectation Alignment Gate**
If candidate's expectation is significantly above FDD data → PAUSE. Have honest conversation before proceeding. Better to lose deal now than have unhappy franchisee later.
If candidate's expectation aligns with FDD reality → PROCEED.

## FDD Qualifier Language (mandatory)
All financial figures must include: "Results may vary. See FDD for details." OR "Per FDD Item 19; results may vary." OR "Illustrative; actual results depend on individual operator, territory, and market conditions."

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker either over-promising or awkwardly avoiding financial conversations.
**Result:** Every candidate enters award with eyes open. Franchisee satisfaction and retention increases.
**Advaita gap closed:** Expectation management — from 0% → 95% AI-autonomous.
