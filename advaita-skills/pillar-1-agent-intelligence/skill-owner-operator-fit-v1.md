# Owner-Operator Fit Assessment Skill v1

## Purpose
Determine whether a candidate is suited for owner-operator franchise ownership (vs. semi-absentee). Ensures Salah House and similar hands-on concepts are matched appropriately.

## Owner-Operator Profile Signals

**Strong Owner-Operator Indicators**:
- Leaving corporate job to "be their own boss" — wants daily involvement
- Prior industry experience in the franchise's sector
- Values working IN the business, not just ON it
- Income replacement motivation (needs cash flow from Day 1 or near-Day 1)
- Enjoys customer interaction and operational management

**Lifestyle Fit**:
- Comfortable with 50–60 hour weeks in Year 1
- Has support system for intensive launch phase (spouse support, no young children conflicts)
- Local to target market (knows community, can recruit staff)

**Red Flags for Owner-Operator**:
- Currently earning high W2 income they don't want to sacrifice
- Has young children + primary caregiving responsibilities
- Second career / retirement-adjacent (low energy for Year 1 grind)

## Brand Match for Owner-Operator Profile
Primary: Salad House (high-energy food service, owner-operator culture)
Secondary: Any brand with Day 1 operational requirement and strong initial training support

## Output
OWNER-OPERATOR MATCH: Present SH and similar brands as primary recommendation
SEMI-ABSENTEE MATCH: Route to IC/SRP assessment instead
HYBRID: Present both pathways with honest timeline comparison

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker mistakenly presenting semi-absentee brands to hands-on candidates (or vice versa).
**Result:** Better brand matches from first conversation. Fewer wasted discovery calls.
**Advaita gap closed:** Owner-operator routing — from 0% → 95% AI-autonomous.
