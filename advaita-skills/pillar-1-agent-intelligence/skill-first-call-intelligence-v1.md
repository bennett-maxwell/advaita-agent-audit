# First Call Intelligence Brief Skill v1

## Purpose
Before any broker makes first contact with a new lead, AI assembles a 60-second intelligence brief: who this person is, what they likely want, and how to open the conversation.

## Intelligence Sources (automated data pull)

**From GHL intake form:**
- Name, location, investment range, stated timeline, source

**From web research (Squirrel executes if lead quality warrants):**
- LinkedIn profile: current role, company, career history
- Company size / industry (signals income, W2 security level)
- Approximate tenure in current role (burnout timing signal)

**From FKI content engagement:**
- Which pages visited (state guide? investment range? specific brand?)
- How many times visited and recency
- Content that drove conversion (SEO, paid, referral)

## Brief Format
```
LEAD: [Name] | [City, State]
BACKGROUND: [Title at Company] — [X years in role] [signals: career stage, income bracket]
INTEREST SIGNAL: Found FKI via [source]. Viewed [pages]. Indicates interest in [inferred category].
RECOMMENDED OPENER: "[Specific personalized opening line referencing their background and likely motivation]"
LIKELY PROFILE: [semi-absentee investor / W2-exit / passive income — based on signals]
BRANDS TO MENTION: [1–2 most likely fit based on profile]
```

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker making cold call with only a name and phone number.
**Result:** Every first contact is warm, personalized, and positioned correctly. First impressions that close deals.
**Advaita gap closed:** First contact preparation — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
