# Spoiled Rotten Photography Buyer Matcher Skill v1

## Purpose
SRP is a distinctive franchise — event-based photography for children and schools. AI matches candidates who will thrive in this specific model.

## SRP Ideal Buyer Profile
- Community-oriented, enjoys working with families and children
- Comfortable with event-based revenue model (seasonal, school calendar-driven)
- Lifestyle business orientation — values flexibility and community visibility over pure income maximization
- Photography interest is a plus but NOT required (operational, not photography, role)
- Investment range: per FDD Item 7 (results may vary; see FDD)

## SRP Revenue Model Clarity (set expectations)
Revenue is event-driven: school photo sessions, sports photos, community events. Seasonal concentration means revenue is NOT evenly distributed through the year. Candidate must be comfortable with revenue seasonality.

## SRP No State Blocks
SRP has no registered state blocks — national availability (verify current territory status with franchisor before presenting).

## SRP Semi-Absentee Assessment
SRP can be operated semi-absentee with the right team, but Year 1 requires significant owner involvement during event season. Assess carefully using skill-semi-absentee-fit-v1.

## Community Fit Questions for SRP
- Do you have or want a visible community presence?
- Are you comfortable with school and family-oriented environment?
- Does working around children's milestones feel meaningful to you?

**Replaces:** SRP being presented to candidates who don't fit the model.
