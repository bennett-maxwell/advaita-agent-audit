# FDD Intake and Synthesis Skill v1

## Purpose
When a candidate receives an FDD, AI extracts the 12 most critical data points and creates a 1-page candidate brief. Removes the FDD overwhelm barrier.

## Critical Extraction Points

| FDD Item | What to Extract | Why It Matters |
|---------|----------------|----------------|
| Item 5 | Initial franchise fee | Capital planning |
| Item 6 | Ongoing royalty % and marketing fee % | P&L modeling |
| Item 7 | Total estimated initial investment range (results may vary, see FDD) | Capital requirements |
| Item 12 | Territory size and exclusivity terms | Market protection |
| Item 15 | Franchisee obligations (owner-operator vs manager-run allowed) | Lifestyle fit |
| Item 19 | Financial performance representations (if disclosed; results may vary) | Revenue benchmarking |
| Item 20 | Number of open/closed/transferred units by state | System health |
| Item 21 | 3-year financial statements | Franchisor stability |
| Item 6 | Transfer fee, renewal terms | Exit planning |
| Item 9 | Franchisee obligations list | Commitment clarity |
| Item 17 | Termination conditions | Risk assessment |
| Item 8 | Restrictions on goods/services/suppliers | Operations flexibility |

## Output: Candidate FDD Brief
- 1-page summary in plain language
- Traffic-light scoring: GREEN (positive) / YELLOW (review needed) / RED (concern)
- 3 most important questions to ask during validation calls
- Comparison to other brands in candidate's shortlist (if applicable)

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker or candidate spending hours reading FDD.
**Result:** Candidate gets clear, actionable summary. Decision velocity increases dramatically.
**Advaita gap closed:** FDD comprehension barrier — from 0% → 85% AI-autonomous.
