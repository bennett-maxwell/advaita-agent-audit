# Spouse and Partner Alignment Skill v1

## Purpose
Proactively manage the spouse/partner alignment dimension of every deal. Most franchise deals die not from financial or brand concerns, but from household misalignment. This skill closes that gap.

## Detection
GHL field: "Is your spouse/partner involved in this decision?" → YES / NO / SOMEWHAT

**If YES (both aligned from Day 1)**: Full speed ahead — standard process.

**If SOMEWHAT**: Immediate action required before investing more discovery time.

**If NO (candidate alone)**: Gentle flag — needs spouse involvement for deal to close.

## Spouse-Positive Sequence

When spouse not involved, AI triggers (candidate-facing, educational):
1. Email: "The most successful franchise families — what the research shows" (supportive, not alarming)
2. Content: "How to start the franchise conversation with your partner" (specific talking points)
3. Offer: "Would it help to have both of you on the next call? No pressure — many couples find this helpful."

## Spouse-on-Call Preparation
When spouse will join:
AI generates spouse-specific brief for broker:
- Common spouse concerns (risk, time, money, "why not just get a job")
- How to address each concern specifically
- What spouse success looks like from a conversation standpoint

## Spouse Red Flag Signals
- Candidate says "my spouse said no" → PAUSE deal; activate spousal re-engagement sequence
- Candidate says "my spouse doesn't know yet" → CRITICAL: do not advance; broker call needed
- Candidate avoids spouse topic consistently → broker must address directly

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker awkwardly handling spouse objections ad hoc.
**Result:** Systematic spousal alignment process. Fewer last-minute deal kills.
**Advaita gap closed:** Household alignment management — from 0% → 80% AI-autonomous.
