# Industry Vertical Matcher Skill v1

## Purpose
Candidate's prior industry experience is a powerful predictor of franchise success AND a key reason they'll feel confident in a brand. AI surfaces this connection proactively.

## Industry → Franchise Vertical Mapping

**Healthcare / Nursing / Pharmacy**
→ Relevant verticals: Health & wellness franchises, senior care, medical staffing, IV therapy, med spa concepts
→ FKI portfolio connection: Semi-absentee health-adjacent brands where clinical background = credibility

**Corporate HR / People Operations**
→ Relevant verticals: Staffing franchises, training/education, employee services
→ FKI portfolio connection: IC (community connection, people-focused management model)

**Sales / Business Development**
→ Relevant verticals: Any franchise with strong sales culture, B2B service franchises
→ FKI portfolio connection: SH (high AUV driven by volume + community sales)

**Operations / Supply Chain / Logistics**
→ Relevant verticals: Service franchises with complex operations, multi-location management
→ FKI portfolio connection: All brands — operations background is valuable in any franchise

**Marketing / Creative / Agency**
→ Relevant verticals: Marketing service franchises, creative services, digital services
→ FKI portfolio connection: SRP (photography + marketing component), IC (community brand building)

**Finance / Accounting**
→ Relevant verticals: Tax services, financial services, insurance
→ Note: Strong analytical = excellent at evaluating FDD and running unit economics model

**Real Estate**
→ Relevant verticals: Property services, restoration, cleaning, inspection
→ FKI portfolio connection: Real estate background = excellent multi-unit territory expansion candidate

## Matching Logic
1. Identify candidate's primary industry from LinkedIn/intake
2. Surface top 2 vertical matches from above
3. Add to discovery call prep brief (skill-discovery-call-prep-v1)
4. Frame connection in recommendation: "Given your background in [X], you'll likely find [Brand] resonates because..."

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker manually making industry connections during conversation.
**Result:** Every recommendation feels personally relevant to candidate's background. Trust builds faster.
**Advaita gap closed:** Industry-informed matching — from 0% → 90% AI-autonomous.
