# Brand Fit Matching Skill v1 (Deep Match)

## Purpose
Beyond financial and geographic filters, AI matches candidate's personality, work style, values, and lifestyle goals to brand culture. Reduces post-award regret and increases franchisee happiness.

## Brand Culture Profiles (FKI Portfolio)

**Indy Clover (IC)**
Culture: Community-first, mission-driven, flexible operations
Ideal owner: Female 28–50, professional background, community-oriented, comfortable delegating day-to-day to manager
Red flags: Candidate wants to be in store daily, pure ROI motivation, no community connection interest
Semi-absentee compatible: YES (manager-run from day 1)
Brand language note: "community hub" — never "thrift store" (HARD RULE)

**Salad House (SH)**
Culture: Hands-on entrepreneurship, food-service excellence, high-volume operations
Ideal owner: Operationally engaged, comfortable in food service environment, investor-oriented with active involvement
Red flags: True passive/absentee only, no food service tolerance, small market preference
Semi-absentee compatible: EVOLVING (possible with strong GM; assess individually)

**Spoiled Rotten Photography (SRP)**
Culture: Creativity, community events, memory-making, lifestyle business
Ideal owner: Values flexibility, wants community visibility, comfortable with event-based revenue model
Red flags: Pure ROI focus, no interest in child/family context, needs predictable W2-style income
Semi-absentee compatible: CHALLENGING without strong team

## Fit Scoring
Each candidate scores 0–10 on culture fit for each brand (based on intake answers + call notes).
Brand recommended only if culture fit score ≥ 7.

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker intuiting cultural fit from conversation.
**Result:** Every recommendation includes cultural fit rationale. Happier franchisees, better validation calls, higher close rates.
**Advaita gap closed:** Culture fit matching — from 0% → 85% AI-autonomous.
