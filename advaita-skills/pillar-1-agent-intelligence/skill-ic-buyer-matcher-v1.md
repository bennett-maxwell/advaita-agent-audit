# Indy Clover Buyer Matcher Skill v1

## Purpose
Indy Clover has a specific ideal buyer profile. AI matches only qualified buyers to IC and ensures brand-specific language and compliance rules are followed.

## IC Ideal Buyer Profile
- Female 28–50 preferred (though not exclusive — verify with FKI)
- W2 professional background
- Semi-absentee ownership intent
- Community orientation and values
- Investment range: per FDD Item 7 (results may vary; see FDD for current figures)
- FDD Item 19 median net performance data available (results may vary; see FDD)

## IC Brand Language Rules (HARD)
- ALWAYS say "community hub" — NEVER "thrift store" (non-negotiable brand language)
- Framing: community impact, not resale goods
- Customer base: community members donating to and shopping at a purpose-driven store

## IC State Blocks (HARD)
IC territories not registered in: TX, NC, SC, FL, KY, ME, OR, UT
If candidate's target state is on this list → IC is removed from recommendation. Period.

## IC Community Fit Assessment
Score candidate 1–10 on:
- Community connection in target market (knows neighbors, involved in local orgs)
- Mission alignment (cares about community impact, not just income)
- Delegation comfort (comfortable hiring/managing store manager)

IC recommendation requires score ≥ 7 on community fit.

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Broker manually applying IC filters.
**Result:** IC only presented to buyers who will thrive. Brand reputation protected. Conversion rates higher.
**Advaita gap closed:** IC-specific matching — from 0% → 100% AI-autonomous.
