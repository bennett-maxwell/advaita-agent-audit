# First-Generation Wealth Builder Buyer Profile Skill v1

## Purpose
First-generation Americans and first-generation wealth builders are among the most motivated and dedicated franchise operators. AI identifies and serves this segment with culturally resonant discovery.

## Profile Signals
- Immigrant background or first-generation college graduate
- Strong family motivation ("building something for my children")
- Wealth-building orientation — franchise as vehicle for generational change
- Risk tolerance is HIGH (relative to total asset base) — understand context
- Community reputation and respect are important (IC brand resonates)

## Discovery Framing
"Franchise ownership has been a path to business ownership and community standing for generations of first-generation entrepreneurs — the system provides what a solo business doesn't: training, support, and a proven model."

## Specific Considerations
Capital structure: First-gen buyers may have non-traditional asset bases (real estate equity in primary residence, family contributions, business cash flow)
Community connection: For IC, first-gen buyers often have deep neighborhood roots — powerful for community hub business
Objection: "I don't have business experience" → Address with franchise-as-training reframe

## Motivational Anchor
"You've built [X] through hard work and discipline. Franchise ownership is the next step — a proven system that rewards exactly those qualities."

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Discovery process that assumes every buyer has corporate career background.
**Result:** First-generation buyers feel recognized and motivated throughout process. High deal commitment.
**Advaita gap closed:** First-generation buyer segment — from 0% → 90% AI-autonomous.
