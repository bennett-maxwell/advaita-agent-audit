# Objection Handling Decision Tree Skill v1

## Purpose
For every common franchise sales objection, provide the AI-recommended response framework. Broker triggers this skill during or after a call to get guidance.

## Top 12 Objections and Response Trees

**OBJ-1: "I don't have enough capital"**
→ Clarify actual liquid capital vs. stated amount
→ Introduce ROBS / SBA / HELOC pathways (consult financial advisor; results vary)
→ Match to brands within actual capital range (per FDD; results may vary)
→ If truly insufficient: honest conversation about timing, 6–12 month savings plan

**OBJ-2: "My spouse isn't on board"**
→ Invite spouse to next call — make it their conversation
→ Address specific spouse concern (risk, time commitment, financial exposure)
→ Share validation call approach: "Let's hear from franchisees in your situation"

**OBJ-3: "I want to think about it more"**
→ Identify WHAT specifically they need to think about (don't let it be vague)
→ Address the specific concern
→ Territory availability urgency: territories are finite
→ Set specific follow-up date — not "I'll reach out"

**OBJ-4: "I'm worried about failure rates"**
→ Direct to FDD Item 20 (units open vs. closed — real data)
→ Validation calls with existing franchisees (FDD Item 20 list)
→ Reframe: franchise reduces DIY startup risk; systematic support exists

**OBJ-5: "The fees seem high"**
→ Reframe as access to proven system, brand, and support (vs. building from scratch)
→ Compare: franchise royalties vs. cost of customer acquisition alone in independent biz
→ ROI modeling based on FDD Item 19 if disclosed (results may vary; see FDD)

**OBJ-6: "I don't have any business experience"**
→ Franchises are specifically designed for people without industry experience
→ Training and support structure exists for this exact situation
→ Connect with franchisees who had zero experience pre-franchise (validation calls)

**OBJ-7: "I'm not sure about the territory"**
→ Run territory viability analysis (activate skill-territory-viability-v1)
→ Present data on territory demographics and opportunity
→ Discuss territory protection terms from FDD Item 12

**OBJ-8: "I need to talk to my accountant/attorney first"**
→ ENCOURAGE this — it's the right move
→ Offer FDD summary to send to their advisors
→ Schedule follow-up call with advisor included if helpful

**OBJ-9: "I've heard bad things about franchises"**
→ Ask: specific brand or general? Address specifically.
→ FDD is public record — all data is disclosed by law
→ Point to FDD Item 21 (franchisor financials) and Item 19 (performance if disclosed)

**OBJ-10: "I'm looking at other options first"**
→ Understand what other options (other franchise? business purchase? stock market?)
→ Don't fight it — ask what criteria they're using to evaluate
→ Position FKI as helping them evaluate ALL options, not just FKI brands

**OBJ-11: "The timeline feels too fast"**
→ Acknowledge — this is a big decision
→ But: territory availability is real constraint
→ Create decision milestone map — specific dates for each step, no pressure between steps

**OBJ-12: "What if I want to sell the franchise later?"**
→ FDD Item 12 (transfer terms) and Item 17 (renewal/termination)
→ Resale value discussion — proven unit performance history drives valuation
→ Connect to resale process skill (future skill)
