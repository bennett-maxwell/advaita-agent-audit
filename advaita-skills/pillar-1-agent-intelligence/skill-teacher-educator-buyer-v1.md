# Teacher and Educator Buyer Profile Skill v1

## Purpose
K–12 teachers, school administrators, and education professionals are a compelling FKI buyer segment — mission-alignment with IC, structured mindset, and summer availability for launch.

## Educator Buyer Profile
- Mission-driven: education → IC community hub mission resonates deeply
- Structured, process-oriented: follows proven systems naturally (franchise fit)
- Community embedded: already known and trusted in their local community
- Summer availability: potential for summer launch phase with full attention
- Income supplement motivation: teacher compensation → franchise = income diversification

## FKI Brand Match
IC: Highest alignment. Community hub in same neighborhoods educators serve. "You already know these families — this business serves them in a different way."
SRP: Photography + school events + community focus → strong mission alignment.

## Capital Profile for Educators
Often lower liquid capital than corporate professionals. Focus on:
- FDD Item 7 investment range verification (results may vary; see FDD)
- Funding paths: ROBS if has 403b/pension, HELOC if homeowner, SBA 7(a) if capital-eligible
- Spouse income often the primary capital source in dual-income households
