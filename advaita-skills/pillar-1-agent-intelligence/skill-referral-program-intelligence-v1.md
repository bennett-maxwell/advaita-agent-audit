# Referral Program Intelligence Skill v1

## Purpose
Systematically identify, track, and activate referral opportunities from existing FKI candidates and franchisees. Referral leads are the highest-quality, highest-close-rate leads FKI generates.

## Referral Trigger Points (AI monitors and fires)

**Post-Discovery Call** (when candidate is excited, pre-FDD):
Auto-trigger: "You mentioned you're talking to friends about this — if anyone comes to mind who'd benefit from this conversation, I'd love to connect with them. No obligation on your end."

**Post-Award** (new franchisee warm glow period):
Auto-trigger: Referral request 30 days post-award. "Who else in your network is in the life stage where this kind of move would resonate?"

**First Unit Open** (franchisee success moment):
Auto-trigger: "Congratulations on your opening! If any of your community or network is curious about what you're building, we'd welcome an introduction."

**Validation Call Contacts** (existing franchisees who speak with candidates):
AI tracks validation contacts and identifies those who proactively mention referrals during calls.

## Referral Tracking in GHL
- Tag: `referral-source-[name]` on referred lead
- Custom field: referrer name on referred lead record
- Attribution tracked through entire pipeline to close

## Referral Recognition (REVERSIBLE)
Track referrals → notify referring party at each milestone (lead qualified, discovery call, award).
Referral program terms: Bennett approves referral incentive structure (IRREVERSIBLE if financial).
