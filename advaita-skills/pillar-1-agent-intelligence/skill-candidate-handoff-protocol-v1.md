# Candidate Handoff Protocol Skill v1

## Purpose
When a candidate moves from one broker to another (vacation, capacity, specialization), AI generates a structured handoff brief and ensures continuity. Zero candidate experience degradation.

## Handoff Trigger Events
- Broker vacation / unavailability (>5 days)
- Candidate geographic specialization (route to broker with expertise in that market)
- Brand specialization (route to broker with deep IC, SH, or SRP knowledge)
- Candidate capacity (high-volume periods, load balancing)

## Handoff Brief (auto-generated by AI)

**Candidate Profile**: Name, contact, location, FranchiseScore, stage in pipeline

**Relationship Summary**: How long working together, tone of relationship, any sensitive topics (spouse concerns, financial stress, etc.)

**Brands in Discussion**: Which brands presented, candidate reaction to each

**Current Status**: Where in process, last touchpoint, what's next

**Open Items**: What the outgoing broker promised to follow up on

**Key Personality Notes**: Communication style preference (text/email/call), best times to reach, what resonates with them

**Do NOT mention**: Any topics the previous broker was handling sensitively

## Handoff Communication to Candidate
AI drafts warm introduction email:
"[Candidate], I wanted to introduce you to [New Broker] who will be your primary contact while I'm [reason]. They're fully briefed on your search and excited to continue the conversation. [New Broker] — [Candidate] is exploring [brands] and is at [stage]."

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Manual broker notes and awkward warm handoffs.
**Result:** Every handoff is seamless. Candidate feels supported, not passed off.
