# Brand Portfolio Expansion Research Skill v1

## Purpose
When considering adding new brands to the FKI portfolio, AI runs structured research protocol to evaluate fit. Removes guesswork from portfolio expansion decisions.

## Evaluation Framework (REVERSIBLE — research and analysis only)

**Step 1 — Brand Basics**
- Franchise concept and category
- Years in franchising
- Current unit count (open, closed, transferred — per FDD Item 20 when available)
- Franchisor financial health (FDD Item 21 when available)

**Step 2 — FKI Buyer Fit**
- Does this brand fit FKI's buyer profiles? (semi-absentee, community-oriented, investment-minded)
- Investment range compatibility with FKI pipeline (per FDD Item 7; results may vary)
- Semi-absentee ownership model: supported or not?

**Step 3 — Broker Economics**
- Commission structure (rates vary by franchisor — verify directly)
- Award process timeline
- Franchisor support for broker relationship

**Step 4 — Differentiation**
- Does this brand add something FKI doesn't already have?
- Geographic coverage expansion (new regions)?
- New vertical (industry category not currently in portfolio)?

**Step 5 — Compliance Check**
- State registration coverage (any blocked states?)
- FDD Item 19 disclosure available? (financial performance representations; results may vary)

## Output: Brand Evaluation Brief
One-page summary covering all 5 dimensions with GO / CONDITIONAL / NO-GO recommendation.
Note: IRREVERSIBLE (adding brand to active portfolio, signing broker agreement) → human gate for Bennett.

## Autonomy Value
**Replaces:** Manual brand research before portfolio conversations.
**Result:** Every new brand opportunity evaluated systematically. Bennett makes portfolio decisions based on structured data.
**Advaita gap closed:** Portfolio research — from 0% → 80% AI-autonomous (final portfolio decision = Bennett gate).
