## Why Franchise Under $300,000?

Lower-investment franchises reduce financial risk while still delivering meaningful returns. Benefits include:

- **Lower breakeven thresholds** — less revenue required to cover fixed costs
- **Faster ROI timelines** — many under-$300K franchises reach profitability within 18–24 months
- **SBA loan eligibility** — most qualify for SBA 7(a) loans, reducing out-of-pocket capital requirements
- **Scalability** — lower initial cost makes multi-unit ownership more accessible
- **Reduced personal financial risk** — fewer assets pledged as collateral

According to the International Franchise Association, small-format and service-based franchises are among the fastest-growing segments in 2026, driven by low overhead, home-based or small-footprint models, and strong consumer demand.

## How to Evaluate a Franchise Under $300,000

When researching affordable franchise opportunities, experienced consultants recommend analyzing:

- **Item 19 (FDD)** — financial performance representations from existing franchisees
- **Franchisee validation** — direct conversations with current owners about real-world profitability
- **Territory exclusivity** — ensure your market is protected from competitor franchisees
- **Royalty structure** — flat fee vs. percentage of revenue, and what that means at scale
- **Support infrastructure** — training depth, marketing support, and ongoing operational guidance
- **Exit multiples** — how the brand is valued if you sell in 5–7 years
