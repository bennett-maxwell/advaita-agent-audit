## What Is Indy Clover?

Indy Clover is a **curated thrift and consignment franchise** built around a community hub model. It's not a thrift store in the traditional sense—Indy Clover franchisees don't buy, hold, or own any inventory. Instead:

- **Sellers rent booth space** and bring their own merchandise
- **Franchisee revenue** comes from booth rental fees—not product margin
- **Franchisee role**: curation, community building, and operations management (not retail)

This model fundamentally changes the risk profile. Traditional retail requires capital tied up in inventory. Indy Clover franchisees collect revenue before they sell a single item.

**Brand positioning**: "You don't own the inventory. You own the community."

## The Business Model: Why Booth Rental Changes Everything

Traditional resale/thrift franchises require the franchisee to purchase inventory, manage it, and hope it sells. Indy Clover's booth rental model flips this:

**Revenue stability**: Monthly booth rental fees provide predictable recurring income regardless of what individual sellers earn.

**No inventory risk**: Franchisee doesn't carry the markdown risk when items don't sell.

**Seller relationship**: The franchisee's job is to attract quality sellers (who bring quality inventory), not to source product.

**Semi-absentee potential**: Once a strong location manager is in place, the owner's daily involvement drops significantly. The brand is designed for 10-20 hours per week owner involvement.

## Who Is the Right Buyer?

Indy Clover's buyer profile is distinctly different from investment-focused franchises:

- **Age range**: 28-55
- **Background**: W2 employed professional who wants an additional income stream
- **Liquid capital**: $70,000–$150,000
- **Time commitment**: Willing to invest 10-20 hours/week (not full-time)
- **Motivation**: Community-driven, wants to create a local gathering place, passionate about sustainability and secondhand culture

**Who it's NOT for**:
- Buyers seeking hands-off passive income (still requires meaningful involvement, especially in early months)
- Buyers without community mindset (the seller relationships are the product)
- States where Indy Clover is not registered (see territorial restrictions below)

## Territorial Restrictions (FDD Compliance)

**Important**: Indy Clover's franchise is not registered in all states. As of the 2026 FDD, the franchise is **NOT registered** (and therefore cannot be offered or sold) in: Texas, North Carolina, South Carolina, Florida, Kentucky, Maine, Oregon, and Utah.

If you reside in or are considering one of these states, you cannot currently pursue an Indy Clover franchise. Contact Franchise KI to discuss alternatives.

## Is Indy Clover Right for You?

Indy Clover is a genuine differentiated concept in the $50B+ resale market. The booth rental model reduces inventory risk, the semi-absentee design is built into the operating system, and SBA approval makes financing accessible.

It's not right for everyone. Buyers who need maximum ROI speed, prefer hands-off ownership, or live in restricted states should explore other options.

For buyers who connect with the community hub vision and have the capital profile, it's worth a serious look.
