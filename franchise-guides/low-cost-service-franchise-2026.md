# Low-Cost Service Franchises Under $100K in 2026: The Best Options

*By FranchiseKI | Updated May 2026 | franchiseki.com*

---

Service franchises are the fastest-growing category in franchising—and for good reason. Unlike food or retail, many service concepts require no expensive real estate, no inventory, and minimal staff to launch. Here are the best service franchises available for under $100K total investment in 2026.

## Why Service Franchises Win on ROI

The math is compelling:

- **No inventory carrying costs**
- **No commercial lease** (many are home-based or van-based)
- **Recurring revenue** from repeat customers
- **Low labor requirements** to start (often owner-operated)
- **Margin-rich** — service gross margins typically 40-70%

Compare this to a food franchise where buildout alone can cost $150K-$400K before you serve your first customer.

## Key Evaluation Factors for Service Franchises

**1. Recurring vs. one-time revenue.** Cleaning, pest control, and senior care generate recurring revenue. Restoration and refinishing are project-based. Both work — but recurring is more predictable.

**2. Seasonality.** Pest control is seasonal. Cleaning is year-round. Understand your cash flow cycles before signing.

**3. Labor dependency.** Can you start owner-operated, or do you need staff day one? Labor is the #1 cost center and operational challenge in service businesses.

**4. Insurance and licensing.** Many service concepts (restoration, senior care) have regulatory requirements by state. Verify your state's licensing requirements before committing.

**5. Territory size.** A protected territory with strong demographics matters more in service franchises than food. Population density and median income drive revenue potential.
