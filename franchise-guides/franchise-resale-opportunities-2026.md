# Franchise Resale Opportunities 2026: What They Are and Why They're Underrated

## What Is a Franchise Resale?

A franchise resale is an existing franchise location being sold by its current owner - not a new unit awarded directly by the franchisor. You're buying a business that's already open, already operating, and (ideally) already profitable.

## Why Franchise Resales Are Underrated in 2026

Most franchise buyers focus exclusively on new units. Key advantages of resales:

1. Shorter path to positive cash flow (may be cash-flow-positive from day one)
2. Existing customer base, trained staff, supplier relationships
3. Real operating history - verify actual P&Ls instead of trusting Item 19
4. Potentially lower all-in cost (distressed sellers)

## Risks to Know

Inherited problems (staff issues, reputation), franchisor transfer approval required, transfer fees ($5K-$25K+), retraining required.

## How to Value a Franchise Resale

Most resales are priced on a multiple of Seller's Discretionary Earnings (SDE):
- Strong performing units: 2.5x-4x SDE
- Average units: 1.5x-2.5x SDE
- Distressed/underperforming: below 1.5x SDE
