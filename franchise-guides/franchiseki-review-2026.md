# FranchiseKI Review 2026: What Clients Actually Experience

If you're researching franchise consultants and you've landed on FranchiseKI, you probably have a few questions: Who are they? How does their process work? What's the catch?

Here's an honest overview of what working with FranchiseKI actually looks like.

## Who Is FranchiseKI?

FranchiseKI is an AI-powered franchise consulting and marketplace platform. We help prospective franchise buyers research, evaluate, and select franchise concepts — and we've built proprietary technology to make the process faster and more data-driven than traditional franchise brokerage.

Our founders built a franchise concept, scaled it nationally, made mistakes (opening in multiple states before systems were ready), and closed locations. That experience — both building from the inside and navigating the consequences — informs every recommendation we make.

We don't name the concept publicly. But the lesson it taught us is the one we apply to every client: **the right system, in the right market, with the right capitalization, run by the right operator. Everything else is details.**

## What FranchiseKI Does

**MapKi:** Our franchise research platform at mapki.franchiseki.com lists 3,200+ brands with verified FDD data. It's free to search.

**FranchiseScore:** Our AI-generated fit score (0-100) for every concept in our database. Like a Zestimate, but for franchise investment quality. Synthesizes Item 19 AUV efficiency, franchisor support, territory protection, financial health, and system growth.

**Consulting:** We match investors with concepts based on capital, lifestyle, market, and risk profile. Our process includes FDD orientation, franchisee call coaching, and Discovery Day preparation.

**Cost to clients:** $0. We're paid by franchisors only if you purchase through us. The franchise fee you pay is identical whether you use FranchiseKI or go direct.

## The FranchiseKI Process

**Step 1 — Discovery Call (30-45 minutes)**
We learn about your capital (liquid + accessible), timeline, lifestyle goals (semi-absentee vs. full-time operator), market geography, and industry interests. We're also honest here: if franchising isn't right for your situation, we'll tell you.

**Step 2 — Concept Shortlist (within 1 week)**
Based on your profile, we present 3-5 concepts that match your criteria with FranchiseScore data, estimated investment ranges, and franchisee validation contact lists.

**Step 3 — FDD Review**
We walk you through the key sections — Item 7 (investment), Item 12 (territory), Item 19 (financial performance), Item 20 (franchisee contacts), Item 21 (franchisor financials). We help you know what to look for and what questions to ask your franchise attorney.

**Step 4 — Franchisee Validation**
We coach you on the right questions for validation calls and help you identify franchisees in comparable markets. This is the most important step — existing franchisees are the most honest source of information in the entire process.

**Step 5 — Discovery Day + Decision**
We facilitate introductions, prep you for Discovery Day, and debrief after. Once you've made your decision, we coordinate the execution process.
